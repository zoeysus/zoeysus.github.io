4 Techniques to Improve Your Life

1. Energy Cleanse

  1. Close your eyes.
  2. Take a deep breath in for 4 seconds. Imagine you are inhaling all the positive energy in the world.
  3. Hold the breath for another 4 seconds. Imagine you are letting the positive energy flow through your body.
  4. Exhale for 6 seconds. Imagine that you are pushing out all the negative energy that your body is holding.
  5. Repeat until your body and mind feel cleansed and re-energized.

2. Gratitude = Greatness

Whenever there is a task that you don't want to do and think "I have to do this...", reframe your mindset and think "I get to do this!"

Ex) "I have to go to school." - "I get to go to school."

Ex) "I have to exercise." - "I get to exercise."

3. Think of One Thing to Look Forward to Everyday

This can be anything from cooking a nice meal, reading a good book, hanging out with your friends...anything that makes you happy and motivates you to live another day!

4. Your Issue < Global Issue

Whenever you want to make a big deal out of your minor issue, just think about what the world has to go through and how small your problem is compared to what's going on in the world. Many suffer from poverty. There is a war going on. Innocent species are dying from climate change. Yet, you are bothered about school works and relationships. Be grateful for what you have, don't dwell on small inconveniences.
